Std 11 and 12 - COMMERCE PROGRAM
This stage is the start of students' career path and 'WE' carefully takes up the responsibility of showing light at each step they take.
Commerce, you cannot live without it. 'WE' takes a realistic approach towards theoretical study.
✓ Mode: Offline & Online
✓ Subjects taught: Math, Accounts, Economics
✓ Sessions: 6 days a week, 1-2 sessions/day, 1.5 hours/session
✓ Duration: 1 year course – from April till final exam
✓ Faculty: Teachers with 5-7 years of experience in methodical and simplified teaching.
Qualified commerce post-graduates
✓ Teaching techniques:

  • Dynamic classroom teaching incorporating current Economy and Business scenarios.
  • Core Curriculum integrated with knowledge of present national financial situations.
  • Students' active participation is encouraged to create a competitive, learning- conducive environment.
  • Study notes and practice assignments are provided.
  • End-of-topic tests that help students take easy bites of their vast syllabus.
  • Feedback sessions and paper discussions are conducted after every test.
  • Solving of past year papers and sample papers is periodically scheduled and assessed.
  • Detailed exam orientation, teamed with individual plan of action for each student.

    • ✓ Co-curricular activities:

  • Students are given projects and activities to make them conversant with the new commerce concepts and experiment their practical applications

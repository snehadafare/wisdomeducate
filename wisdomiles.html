WISDOMILES
Direct all the energies within a child towards a positive pursuance of his goals.

An Aptitude Test to assess the Aptitude in different streams such as engineering, medical, pharmacy, law, armed forces, designing- fashion & product, architecture, journalism. Each field of interest expects the development of specific skill sets.

WisdoMiles comprises of the question types that cover all streams. Attempting these varied question types enables students to know both better- the streams and their aptitude.

✓ Mode: Online
✓ Subjects included: Math, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Current Affairs, Mental Ability, Verbal Ability, Logical Reasoning
✓ Sessions: Every year in the month of January
✓ Coverage: Grade 5 to Grade 10
✓ Benefits:

  • WisdoMiles makes smooth the transition from school studies to professional studies.
  • Students who consistently take these tests each year get better orientation to their career path.
  • Periodic Assignments and support videos (video teaching) are incorporated.

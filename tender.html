Std 1 to 4
This is the stage of first impressions of varied subjects, and 'WE' gives its best to make them memorable. The preciousness of these years of learning is reflected in the carefully planned teaching methodology.
✓ Subjects taught: Math, Science, English, Social Studies
✓ Sessions: 6 days a week, 1-2 sessions/day, 1.5 hours/session
✓ Duration: 10-11 months' course - From June till final exams
✓ Faculty: Teachers over 5 years' experience, qualified with Early Childhood & Development Certification.
✓ Teaching techniques:

  • Adequate writing and learning practice.
  • Special emphasis on developing reading skills, phonetics and spellings, and handwriting.
  • Constant monitoring and guidance. Personalized attention to each student.
  • Easy learning by way of day-to-day life examples

    • ✓ Co-curricular activities:

  • Verbal assessments
  • Group activities and workshops
  • Quizzes, puzzles for joyful learnings
